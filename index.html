Billing Automation for the Entire Subscription Lifecycle of your Business from Initial Offer to Cash in the Bank to Renewals

November 23, 2011

 
Subscription businesses must develop and then refine pricing that is attractive to customers while maximizing revenue. This generally involves going beyond simple recurring fee structures to managing strategic bundling, feature addons, upgrades and downgrades, usage billing and calculating overages. Without automation this becomes a complex, time consuming and often error prone accounting task.

Create Plans and Pricing Rules: Define your services and bundle them into plans. Monexa supports one-time fees, recurring fees and usage based (metered) fee calculation. We also support your free trials.
Acquire and Manage Customers: Use Monexa as the hub for all customer interactions. On-board new accounts, assign service plans, set contract terms, payment frequency and payment method. Monexa then manages the critical billing, communications and contract events automatically.
Turn Metered Data into Billable Line Items: Monexa Billing takes in your metered data and applies it to your service price scales to generate accurate line item billing each period.
Streamline Billing Operations: Issue customized invoices by mail, email or in a portal. Automate payment collection with credit cards, ACH or PayPal. Manage failed payments automatically with our payment rules engine to significantly reduce the need for manual collections. Monexa can automate payments in almost any currency and country.
Enhance Your Brand and Increase Customer Satisfaction with a Self Service Portal: Monexa provides a fully featured, brandable customer self service portal where your customers can view and edit any portion of their account data including payment information, view invoices and payments, view and upgrade services, view usage if a metered services is involved, make a payment, request help when needed and much more.
Report on Critical Business Metrics: Monexa exposes your data in a data warehouse and also provides real-time reporting on the crucial metrics of your business.
Your Business: Manage multiple brands, multiple product lines and multiple resellers. Monexa's hierarchical structure is unparallelled.
Unparalleled API Access: Every function of Monexa Billing is available through the API, providing maximum flexibility. Connect your storefront to on-board customers, set up your customer self service portal to enhance customer service and reduce support calls, Set up automated API to API service provisioning, feed in usage data, query for account status, integrate with other systems, pull other data you need.
Reliable, Scalable Secure: Monexa customers include many large enterprises who accept nothing less than scalable, highly available systems. Monexa has the expertise to handle the integration and data requirements of any size of business and we maintain the highest PCI certification available as well as SSAE16 level II (enhanced Sas70) certification.
Pricing for Every Size of Business: Feature Rich and affordable. Monexa understands the economics of your business. We display our standard pricing on our website or will provide custom pricing to match your business size and strategy
